Economy Game 2.0 - Teaching Innovation
(with Martin Strobel and Chris van der Lans) [GitHub project]

We develop a market simulation tool (oTree, JS, HTML) enabling students to live stylized market settings. Via a smartphone-compatible, browser based platform students trade virtual goods with their student fellows subject to manifold market environments. Supplementing conventional teaching, such first hand-experience opens a different route to understanding of canonical models in microeconomics such as consumer theory, producer theory, partial equilibrium, general equilibrium and welfare.

To experience yourself, we provide a demo version: social planner screen (i.e. admin page) , seller screen , buyer screen.
Please open the admin page first, and in keep it open. Real-time interaction of buyers and sellers relies on the admin page to be opened.
